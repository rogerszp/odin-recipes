Lasagna



A quick and easy lasagna recipe that's perfect for beginners

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  3. Add a layer of lasagna noodles.
  4. Spread ricotta cheese on the noodles, then top with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.
  5. Repeat layers
  6. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on the top layer
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foild and back for another 10 minutes
  8. Let cool for 5 minutes before serving
